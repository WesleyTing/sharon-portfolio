Overview

Homable is an ios app for users to find hotel and hostel accommodations for their travel. It enables users to book hotels, track their trip and record their past travels.

User Research

1. Analyze 30+ hotel, travel, b&b, housing or rental ios apps to find similarities and differences as reference

2. Qualitative interviews among 5 people which seperate two main target groups: tourists and business traveler. Similarities can be reflected as convenient, comfortable, customized, professional but also trendy. Compared with the current main hotel booking apps, I find most of them using sky blue to dark blue as their theme color and many of them have too many stuff on their interface which makes customers feel overwhelmed so based on this I decided initial plan for color and style
