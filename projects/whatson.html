Overview

WhatsOn is an online event booking mobile app that aims at providing convenient and direct ticket service to users.

Discover

I collected insights from 20 qualitative survey and 5 qualitative interviews.

Insights

Over 81% people choose to go to Show or Music as their ideal local event.

55% people decide to go to an event that is referred by friends and families.

Theme and Price (63%) affect people's decision.

People really need clear information display about the event (55%).

"Sometimes I find the information is very scattered. If there is an app that has all the event (information)."
